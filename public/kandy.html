How to use Kandy Voice.

Voice API Build seamless voice applications with IP-2-IP voice calls and full WebRTC SIP trunking functionality. Also make and receive calls to and from international PSTN numbers using Kandy’s voice API. Check out fring for Android and Apple, a mobile app that uses Kandy’s voice API features to allow voice calls to in-app friends for free. fring also provides free group video and text as well as low cost calls to mobile and landline phones. Voice Click-to-Call Click-to-call allows users to immediately connect in real-time, with just a simple click. Kandy supports click-to-call for voice communication on any device – smartphone, tablet, or PC or MAC web browser. Click-to-call allows users to utilize every important second and eliminates wasted time. See how SAP integrates Kandy to deliver their Cloud for Customer platform, enabling sales, service, and business professionals to easily chat, talk, and collaborate in real time with their customers or co-workers.

How to use Kandy Video.
Voice API Easily integrate high quality, low-latency video with Kandy’s video API. By simply integrating Kandy’s video API, users can collaborate more efficiently through the webRTC video functions. Kandy’s webRTC video feature enables multi-party conversations while allowing users the ability to connect by hearing more than just a voice. Check out how Toy Genius embeds Kandy’s video API to offer customers a virtual VIP shopping experience, allowing an expert to find you the perfect gift out of thousands of products.
